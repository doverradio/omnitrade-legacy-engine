
# AI_RESEARCH_AND_EVOLUTION_ENGINE.md

## Vision

OmniTrade is not a single AI trading bot.

It is an Autonomous Capital Management Platform where specialized AI researchers
compete, collaborate, critique, and evolve under strict governance. Researchers
never execute trades directly. They generate evidence-backed recommendations that
flow through replay, validation, risk review, human approval, and controlled
promotion.

---

# Core Principles

1. Research never executes capital.
2. Risk Engine always has final authority.
3. Human approval governs production promotion.
4. Every recommendation is versioned.
5. Every recommendation is reproducible.
6. Every recommendation has lineage.
7. Nothing is silently overwritten.
8. Historical evidence is append-only.

# Strategy Versioning

Strategies are immutable.

Changes create:

- Parent Strategy
- Child Strategy
- Version lineage
- Promotion record
- Rollback path

Nothing replaces production in-place.

---

# Arena Competition

Researchers are ranked using metrics such as:

- Decision Quality
- Expected Value
- Sharpe Ratio
- Drawdown
- Calibration
- Prediction Accuracy
- Capital Efficiency
- Stability

Winning researchers receive additional research budget—not direct trading authority.

---

# Memory

Persist forever:

- hypotheses
- experiments
- recommendations
- critiques
- approvals
- failures
- counterfactual analysis
- production outcomes

Knowledge accumulates permanently.

---

# Promotion Governance

Every production change requires:

- replay
- backtest
- paper validation
- risk approval
- human approval
- audit record

---

# Safety

Researchers may NEVER:

- execute trades
- move capital
- bypass Risk Engine
- delete evidence
- rewrite historical records

---

# Long-Term Vision

OmniTrade becomes an evolving investment research organization rather than a
single trading algorithm. Thousands of specialized AI agents compete to improve
capital allocation while remaining governed by evidence, auditability, risk
controls, and human oversight.
